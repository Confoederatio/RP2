
**Help (Page 2):**

```
@transfer [num] <resources> - Transfer resources to the Antarctic. 10 Resources per icebreaker, per turn.
@evacuate - Evacuate as many people as you can.
@enact <law> - Enact specific laws or policies.

@cook [num] - Cook 1000 rations for every 10 raw_food you have in your inventory.
@refine [num] - Refine 1 steel for every 1 coal and 1 iron you have in your inventory.

@auto-cook - Toggle auto-cook.
```
